About Figueroa Construction Inc.

Figueroa Construction Inc. has been building and remodeling residential and commercial properties around the Denver Metro area since 2001. Our team is specialized in general contracting, restoration work, and is dedicated in providing quality work for all different types of projects. Our construction experience, commitment to service, efficiency, and flexible and creative problem-solving abilities ensure you'll be as excited as we are with a construction project that makes your environment feel brand new. Figueroa Construction Inc. is an insured and bonded company.

Rudy Figueroa, President, Project Manager

Rudy has over 30 years of experience in construction and restoration company management. He founded Figueroa Construction Inc. in 2001 after moving from Orange County, California. Rudy speaks Spanish and English and is a general license contractor in Denver, Aurora, and many other surrounding cities in Denver. He is married and has two children. He enjoys traveling to Sayulita, Mexico and spending time with his family and dogs.
